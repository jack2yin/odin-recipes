Meat Lasagna

This lasagna recipe takes a little work, but it is so satisfying and filling that it's worth it!

Ingredients

  • Meat: Italian sausage and lean ground beef
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products: Crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, and tomato paste
  • White Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings: Parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses: Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Layer the lasagna
  4. Cover with foil and bake
  5. Let the lasagna rest before serving
