Vietnamese Iced Coffee

A strong Vietnamese iced coffee recipe with sweetened condensed milk. Chilled on ice, this makes an unbeatable Southeast Asian treat

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of water
  • 0.5 cup dark roast ground coffee
  • 0.5 sweetened condensed milk
  • Ice cubes

Steps

  1. Brew water with coffee
  2. Spoon 2 table spoons sweetened condensed milk into a cup
  3. Pour 1 cup fresh hot coffee into cup and stir to dissolve milk
  4. Serve coffee along with ice cubes on the side and a long-handle spoon
  5. Pour coffee over ice cubes and stir briskly with long-handled spoon
