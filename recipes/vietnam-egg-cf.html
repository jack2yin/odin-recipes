Vietnamese Egg Coffee

Whipping an egg yolk into sweetened condensed milk will give you a meringue-like, silky, smooth fluff. Top your coffee or iced coffee with this topping and you will have a Vietnamese specialty coffee.

Ingredients

  • 1 large egg yolk
  • 2 tablespoons sweetened condensed milk
  • 0.5 cup hot strong coffee

Steps

  1. Place egg yolk and condensed milk in a tall jar. Whip with a milk frother until soft peaks form
  2. Pour hot coffee into a mug and top with frothed milk
